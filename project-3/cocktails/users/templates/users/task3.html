Product Idea 3

You are a creative entrepreneur looking to generate new product ideas to combat issues of excessive screen time among Amazon Mechanical Turk workers. The product could be a physical product, software or service. The ideas are just ideas. The product need not yet exist, nor may it necessarily be clearly feasible. Give your product a name. You will generate 5 creative ideas iteratively. The product description after the product name should be expressed as a paragraph of 50 - 200 words.
